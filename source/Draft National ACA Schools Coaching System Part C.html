MORE ABOUT THE SUSTAINABLE COACH SYSTEM:

SUSTAINABLE CLUB COACHES:

Based around the Schools and Active After-School Community Programs, as part of a Club’s Whole of Life Coaching and Care, we also need to ensure we have the capacity to continue to provide our services.  So, I am proposing to encourage Clubs to empower themselves to implement a Sustainable Coach System.  Proceeds from Schools, Active After Care and general Coaching will be paid to provide a stream of income to a Coach, who will preferably be a young   person learning Sports Coaching and Management.  Some duties of Marketing and Management can also be taken up by such a Club Coach.  The Club would support the ‘business’ side of being a Coach, and also provide help where needed.

The State Director of Coaching and Sports Development Officer would provide Professional Development as needed.

THE POSSIBILITIES
20 Students at $5.00 per each :  $100
Coach gets $80. 
Coach can do 3 sessions per day for 3 days 80X3X3 : $720
And some Active After Schools at $80 per session.

It may be, that the Coach will get $4.00, a Volunteer or Club $1, up to an agreed figure, equal to the Coaches Award or whatever a Club and Coach contracts them to receive after which the percentages change.  Additionally, the ACA should be taking a $1 for the costs of the Program and System, and for an Equalisation Scheme. So, the coach may receive $3.00 and the Club $1, and the ACA $1.

POSSIBLE CLUB EQUALISATION SCHEME

If the Sustainability System works, we could find some Clubs have lots of Schools to work with, and others have few.  Some Clubs could work on a regional basis, and have a Coach for a larger area.

Some of this may need seeding, and also, ACA would have costs in its provision of a National Director of Coaching and the work undertaken.

So, ACA may need to take some of the earnings to ensure that less well resourced clubs can be assisted.  This spirit of sharing is going to be needed in many sports, which have not yet seen the problem.

THE CLUB AND COACH RELATIONSHIP

Overview

We recognise that the amount of Coaching a player needs to complete the Malletsports Pathway might be between 40 or more Coached sessions, and up to 200 self directed sessions.  No organisation, despite its desire to fulfill a Duty of Care, can provide that for nothing.
After a point where a player decides to Specialise or grow skills beyond a Club level, Coaches can charge.

However, to offset this, Clubs are encouraged to set up systems of Sustainability for Coaches, by allowing Coaches to run Schools and Active After Schools Coaches and use the money as a stream of income for themselves.  VCA wants to encourage Club to empower themselves to help young Coaches ‘run a Coaching business’ within the Club.

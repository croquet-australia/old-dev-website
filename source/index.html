Latest News

[4 Jun] The Australian Tournament Regulations have been amended. Changes have been made to Clause 16.3 and 16.4 to bring Croquet Australia in line with both England and New Zealand. Document Change Tracking
[2 Jun]  Last week, interviews were conducted by a panel from the Executive for the roles of HPM  GC and HPM  AC.  The Executive is delighted to announce that Greg Bury has been appointed to the position of HPM GC and Peter Landrebe to  HPM AC.    The Executive would like to extend their congratulations to both Greg and Peter and are looking forwarding to seeing their endeavours as they work closely with players who are preparing for international competitions whether it be individually or as a member of team representing Australia.
